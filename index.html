Tick Tock Wordle

How To Play

Guess the word in 6 tries in less than 1 minute, Can you do it?

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word
  • You have the option to use a hint by clicking the "Hint" button. Each row can only use one hint.
  • Hints will reveal a letter from the secret word in a random order
